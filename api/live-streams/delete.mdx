---
title: "Delete Live Stream"
api: "DELETE /v1/live-streams/{id}"
description: "Permanently delete a live stream and remove it from the feed."
---

Deletes a live stream and its underlying content item. If the stream is still `active` when you delete it, the broadcast is ended first. Streaming and playback URLs are revoked. Authenticated with your secret key.

The delete is permanent — the stream is excluded from `GET /v1/live-streams`, `GET /v1/live-streams/{id}`, and the clips-listing endpoint from that point on.

Clips already created from the stream are preserved as standalone content items. Historical viewer metrics for the stream also remain available in analytics.

## Path parameters

<ParamField path="id" type="string" required>
  The live stream's UUID.
</ParamField>
