ShortKit is an SDK and infrastructure platform that gives your app a TikTok-quality short-form video feed. You add the package, pass your API key, and get a full-screen swipeable feed with video playback, preloading, deep optimization, recommendation systems, and engagement tracking - all handled for you.

## Where video gets tough

A video feed that *feels* good is deceptively complex. Teams that build this in-house spend thousands of engineering hours on problems like:

- **Player lifecycle.** Mobile platforms impose hard limits on concurrent hardware decoder instances. Exceed them and playback crashes. You need a pool of recycled players with careful teardown, warmup, and handoff on every swipe.
- **Preloading.** The next video should be ready before the user swipes, but prefetching too aggressively wastes bandwidth and starves the video that's currently playing. Research shows 40-60% of preloaded bytes are never watched.
- **Caching.** Native video players don't cache HLS streams out of the box. Without a caching layer, every revisit or loop re-downloads from the CDN.
- **Startup latency.** Each additional second of video load time increases viewer abandonment by roughly 6%. Fast first-frame display requires work that starts well before the user navigates to the video.
- **Main thread pressure.** Video decoding, layout, and rendering all compete for the main thread. During a scroll gesture, dropped frames are immediately visible.
- **Transcoding.** Raw uploads need to be encoded into multiple HLS renditions at different bitrates and resolutions, with proper keyframe alignment, so adaptive streaming works across device types and network conditions.
- **Recommendation systems.** Chronological feeds are easy. Feeds that surface the right content to the right user require engagement tracking, scoring models, and infrastructure that evolves as your data grows.

ShortKit handles all of this. You focus on your content and your app experience.

## How it works

You upload content — videos, image carousels, or surveys — through the [API](/api/overview) or the [admin portal](https://portal.shortkit.dev). ShortKit processes and hosts everything: transcoding, CDN delivery, HLS streaming, and auto-generated captions.

On the client side, the SDK fetches your feed, manages playback, and tracks engagement automatically. Your server never touches video bytes.

## What you can build

- **Full-screen feeds** — A TikTok-style vertical swipe experience that takes over the screen.
- **Partial-height feeds** — A feed embedded in part of a screen, leaving room for your own tab bar or UI.
- **Single-player widgets** — A standalone video player for hero sections, product pages, or inline previews.
- **Horizontal carousels** — A row of video cards for home screens and discovery surfaces.
- **Custom overlays** — Your own UI (titles, buttons, share sheets) rendered on top of the video with full access to playback state.
