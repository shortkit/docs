shortkit is a full-stack platform that enables media companies, news publishers, and content-driven businesses to deploy native, high-quality short-form video feeds within their own mobile and web applications.

## Why shortkit?

Media companies create enormous volumes of content but distribute it primarily on centralized platforms. This creates three structural problems:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Attention leakage" icon="eye-slash">
    Users consume your content but remain on TikTok or Instagram. You build audience for them, not yourself.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Revenue leakage" icon="money-bill-transfer">
    Ad revenue from short-form video accrues to the platform, not you. Your monetization depends on users being in your app.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Data loss" icon="database">
    Engagement signals stay with centralized platforms. You can't use this data to improve your products or sell targeted advertising.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## The shortkit platform

shortkit delivers three tightly integrated components:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Client SDK" icon="mobile" href="/sdk/overview">
    Cross-platform SDK with production-grade vertical video feeds, adaptive bitrate streaming, intelligent prefetch, and extensible UI components.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Video infrastructure" icon="server" href="/guides/platform/content-pipeline">
    Managed backend handling upload, transcoding, CDN delivery, feed ranking, analytics, and metadata management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin portal" icon="browser" href="/portal/overview">
    Web dashboard for content management, feed configuration, A/B testing, ad integration, and analytics.
  </Card>
</Columns>
