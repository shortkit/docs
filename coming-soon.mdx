These features are in active development or planning. Reach out if any of these are critical to your integration — we can share timelines and early access.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Livestreaming" icon="tower-broadcast">
    Stream live video from your app and inject live streams directly into your feed. Support for both broadcasting from client devices and pulling in external live sources.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Fallback URLs" icon="link">
    Still migrating your video library to ShortKit? Fallback URLs let the SDK play back from your existing hosting while you transition. Videos play from your URLs with ShortKit's feed experience on top — though playback quality will depend on your source encoding and CDN.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Advanced Recommendations" icon="brain-circuit">
    Move beyond reverse-chronological feeds with collaborative filtering, two-tower models, and engagement-weighted ranking. Personalized feeds that learn what each user wants to watch.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Overlay Templates" icon="layer-group">
    Pre-built overlay designs you can drop in without writing custom UI. Title cards, like/share buttons, progress bars, and more — styled and ready to go.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
