title: Create survey
description: >-
  Create an in-feed survey with a `question`, answer `options` (each with `text`
  and `position`), and `placementRules` controlling when it appears. Surveys
  start in `draft` status unless you pass `status: "active"`. Only `active`
  surveys are injected into the feed: `draft` is the initial composing state,
  `paused` removes the survey from the feed while preserving its response data,
  and `archived` is a permanently retired state reachable only by deleting the
  survey. `placementRules` must include at least one `min_videos_before` rule
  with a non-negative integer `n`. Supported rule types and their behavior:
  `min_videos_before` (`ruleValue` `{ "n": 3 }`, required) requires the user to
  scroll past at least `n` content items before the survey can appear;
  `once_per_user` (`ruleValue` `{}`) means a user who has already responded
  never sees the survey again; `once_per_session` (`ruleValue` `{}`) shows the
  survey at most once per session regardless of response; `max_per_session`
  (`ruleValue` `{ "n": 1 }`) shows the survey up to `n` times per session. The
  feed evaluates per-user and per-session rules using the `X-User-Id` and
  `X-Session-Id` headers the SDK sends — if these headers are missing,
  `once_per_user`, `once_per_session`, and `max_per_session` are skipped. When
  multiple active surveys are eligible for the same feed slot, the survey with
  the highest `priority` wins.
