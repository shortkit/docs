---
title: "Overview"
description: "Drop short-form video into your app with the ShortKit feed SDK and manage content with the REST API."
---

ShortKit is drop-in short-form video for your app. You add a feed component to your screen, and ShortKit handles the rest: vertical paging, preloading, playback, adaptive streaming, analytics, and content delivery. Your team manages videos, carousels, and live streams through the dashboard or the REST API.

There are two halves to the product:

- **The feed SDK** renders a full-featured short-form video feed inside your app. It ships on five platforms — iOS, Android, Flutter, React Native, and Web — with a consistent API surface and native playback on each.
- **The REST API** is the server-side interface for uploading and importing content, managing publication, creating live streams, receiving webhooks, and querying feeds.
